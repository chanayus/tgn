Black Journey “Beyond Food: Design Life”

Black Journey explores the "taste of place". “Beyond Food, Design Life” philosophy refers to the unique and distinctive flavors, foods, and culinary traditions associated with a specific geographical location or region. It is a concept deeply rooted in the idea that the natural environment, climate, soil, agriculture, and cultural influences of a particular place contribute to the development of flavors and culinary practices that are specific to that area.

Black Journey will take you to places, exploring the favours of Thai and understanding the concept of true Thai terroir. Terroir encompasses the environmental factors that shape the characteristics of a particular food or beverage, such as the soil, climate, and topography of a region.
