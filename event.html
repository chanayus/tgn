Flavor Crafted : Trat Edition

Embark on a journey where the essence of Trat's culinary heritage is not just tasted but experienced. The "Flavour Crafted: Trat Edition" under the Gastronomad campaign invites you to delve deep into the heart of Thailand's eastern province, where every dish tells a story, and every flavor is a testament to generations of tradition.

A Culinary Odyssey Begins

Trat, a province often overshadowed by its more frequented neighbors, holds a treasure trove of gastronomic wonders waiting to be discovered. Here, the rhythm of life is dictated by the tides, the seasons, and the bountiful produce that the land and sea generously offer. The "Flavour Crafted" experience is more than a culinary tour; it's an immersion into the soul of Trat's food culture.

Crafting Your Own Afternoon Tea

8.30 p.m. - 11 p.m.

Imagine sitting by a serene riverside, the gentle breeze carrying the scent of blooming herbs and freshly brewed tea. In this setting, you are not just a guest but a creator. Select from an array of locally sourced ingredients—lemongrass, pandan leaves, butterfly pea flowers—and craft your personalized tea blend. Pair it with traditional sweets.

8.30 p.m. - 11 p.m.
